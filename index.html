üó≥ Committee Election Guidelines

Pros
  • Fair block representation
  • Prevents role capture
  • Encourages participation
  • Supports accountability
  • Each block/tower elects one representative per committee.
  • Term length: 2 years.
  • Staggered rotation preferred.
  • Only owners or registered residents eligible.
  • Maximum two committees per person.
  • Election via ballot (digital or paper).
  • Minimum 50% block turnout required.
  • Mid-term recall by 60% block vote.
  • Results publicly recorded.
